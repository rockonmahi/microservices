About Samradhi

Samraddhi Fish Farms is a Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh based Aquaculture company which do Fish farming (Pond Farming, Biofloc pond Farming, RAS & Biofloc) and are leading CP Premium fish feed authorized distributor of entire Madhya Pradesh.

In association with RM Fisheries Aquaculture, the company also provides Fish Farming Consultancy and online as well as on-farm pure practical training in most simplified possible way. Team of Samraddhi Fish Farm is a great combination of both technical as well as commercial expertise.

After going through all the odds since past 7 years, we have created balanced and sustainable aquaculture practices for everyone out there. Join with us in this amazing journey as we always believe in team work.
